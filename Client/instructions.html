Instructions

  1. How to Connect?
    • Player 1 must go here
    • Player 2 must connect to the same site. Link is here
  2. How to play?
    • Both players can move at the same time with no input lag
    • Players have the option of send different types of mobs or play turrets down on their individual side of the map
    • Every time a minion or turret is placed, gold is spent. Every time a player destroy a minion or turret they receive gold.
    • Each player has access to use a Tank Minion that can shoot at enemy turrets. However, this minion has a cooldown and cost a substantial amount of gold.
    • Below is a table with all of the values for deploying and destroying
    Item  Cost Reward
    Regular Minion 5 7
    Tank 35 50
    Super Minion  50 70
    Turret 15 n/a
  3. How to win?
    • Player must get other player's health to 0 to win the game
